Ocean Freight:

We work on all international sea routes in order to guarantee to our customers a complete global coverage for the traffic of their goods.


The Service includes:

  • - Low Container Load
  • - Contenedores
  • - Break Bulk
  • - OOG Loads
  • - Reefer & Perishables
  • - Bulk Cargo
  • - Flexitank
  • - Industrial Proyects
  • - Stuffing / Unstuffing
  • - Cargo Inspections
  • - IMO
  • - Tank Containers
  • - Door to door
  • - Custom brokerage

Overland

Short, medium and long distance services, with units according to each specific need. Each unit has Global Positioning System, provided and administered by certified company. Our operations department coordinates, manages and tracks each trip and informs to our customers of the status of their cargo at every time.


Service options:

  • - LTL (Less than Truck Load)
  • - FTL (Full Truck Load)
  • - Oversized/OOG Carriage
  • - Heavy Haul
  • - Customs Brokerage services
  • - Drop & Pick Services
  • - Clip On & Underslung Generator Sets

Air Freight

Our air freight service covers to and from any airport in the world. Our specialists coordinate your air transport in order to minimize transit time, optimize the cost of transport and ensure the integrity of the goods in our custody, reporting accurately and detailed the movement of your cargo all the way.


The Service includes:

  • - Pick-up, delivery, and distribution.
  • - Multimodal solutions (combining with land and sea freight sevice)
  • - Documentation management
  • - Customs clearance and brokerage
  • - Special handling
  • - Stuffing / Unstuffing Service

CUSTOM BROKERAGE

- Advice and requirements for each shipment.

- Advice in customs Law.

- Tariff clasification for all kind of merchandise.

Import and Export Destinations (pemanent, temporary, in consignation, transit, transhipments, transfers, reloading, personal effects, postal entrusts.)

Procedures with public agencies (Secretaria de Industria y Comercio , SENASA, IASCAV, INAL, ANMAT, Chambers, etc.)

Payment of Customs taxes and fees, and any kind of payment linked to the operation.

Operations in La Plata and Colonia Free trade zone.


TOTAL COVERAGE

Transport insurance covers the transported goods of all risks that may arise during the voyage such as theft, breakage, loss, depots, strikes, wars, general average, etc.
